[1:00 — ARCHITECTURE]
(show assets/architecture_diagram.png)
Let's walk through the architecture. Incoming leads first hit our Security Checkpoint node, which scrubs PII and checks for prompt injections or competitor domains. If clean, the lead routes to our Orchestrator Agent. The orchestrator delegates work via AgentTools to two specialized sub-agents: the Lead Enricher, which calls MCP tools like lookup_company, check_crm_status, and verify_domain_reputation; and the Lead Scorer, which evaluates the company size and domain fit to calculate a score from 0 to 100. From there, the lead goes to the Decision Node. If it's a high-scoring Hot Lead, the graph pauses for a Human Approval check. If approved, the Sales Router sends the lead to priority sales and outputs a report.
